Trying out osmos system

Dec 18, 2019

Similar to osu!'s pp system, we are using a point system to rank players globally.

Starting from today, you can see how many osmos you would gain from every score. When we will have enough ranked maps, the sum of your earned osmos from a set amount of your best plays on ranked maps will be displayed on your profile, and used to compete between players.

The osmos amount is currently displayed where the Ex score was before.
